Smokey Maple BBQ Chicken Pizza


A sweet and smoky BBQ chicken pizza with a maple-infused sauce, topped with mozzarella, gouda, and fresh cilantro for a mouthwatering flavor explosion.

Serves 2-3 people (1 large pizza)

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough: 1 ball (store-bought or homemade)
  • BBQ Sauce: ½ cup (mix ¼ cup smoky BBQ sauce + 2 tbsp maple syrup)
  • Chicken Breast: 1 cup, cooked and shredded
  • Red Onion: ½, thinly sliced
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 1 ½ cups, shredded
  • Smoked Gouda Cheese: ½ cup, shredded
  • Fresh Cilantro: 2 tbsp, chopped
  • Olive Oil: 1 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper: To taste

Equipment

  • Oven
  • Pizza stone or baking sheet
  • Pizza peel or cutting board
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Small bowl (for BBQ sauce mixture)

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Set to 475°F (245°C) and place your pizza stone or baking sheet inside to preheat.
  2. Prepare Dough: Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness and place it on parchment paper, pizza peel, or cutting board.
  3. Mix BBQ Sauce: In a small bowl, combine smoky BBQ sauce and maple syrup.
  4. Assemble Pizza:
    • Brush olive oil over the dough.
    • Spread the BBQ sauce evenly on the dough.
    • Top with shredded chicken, red onions, mozzarella, and smoked gouda.
  5. Bake: Transfer pizza to the preheated stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. Finish: Remove from the oven, sprinkle with fresh cilantro, slice, and serve!

Truffle Honey Ricotta Delight


A creamy, luxurious white pizza with ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan, finished with truffle oil and a honey drizzle. Perfect for those who love indulgent flavors.

Serves 2 people (1 large pizza)

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough: 1 ball (store-bought or homemade)
  • Ricotta Cheese: 1 cup
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 1 cup, shredded
  • Parmesan Cheese: ¼ cup, grated
  • Truffle Oil: 1 tbsp
  • Honey: 2 tbsp
  • Fresh Thyme: 1 tsp, chopped
  • Black Pepper & Salt: To taste
  • Olive Oil: 1 tbsp
  • Garlic Powder: ½ tsp

Equipment

  • Oven
  • Pizza stone or baking sheet
  • Pizza peel or cutting board
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Small bowl (for mixing)

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Set to 475°F (245°C) and place your pizza stone or baking sheet inside to preheat.
  2. Prepare Dough: Roll out the dough on a floured surface. Brush with olive oil and sprinkle with garlic powder.
  3. Assemble Pizza:
    • Spread ricotta cheese evenly over the dough.
    • Add mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
    • Drizzle with ½ tablespoon of truffle oil.
  4. Bake: Transfer to the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  5. Finish: Remove from the oven, sprinkle with fresh cilantro, slice, and serve!

Spicy Mediterranean Firecracker


A fiery, Mediterranean-inspired pizza with spicy sausage, feta cheese, kalamata olives, and roasted red peppers for a bold and satisfying flavor profile.

Serves 2-3 people (1 large pizza)

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough: 1 ball (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato Sauce: ½ cup
  • Spicy Italian Sausage: 1 cup, cooked and crumbled
  • Feta Cheese: ½ cup, crumbled
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 1 cup, shredded
  • Roasted Red Peppers: ½ cup, sliced
  • Kalamata Olives: ¼ cup, sliced
  • Red Pepper Flakes: ½ tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Oregano: 1 tsp, dried
  • Olive Oil: 1 tbsp

Equipment

  • Oven
  • Pizza stone or baking sheet
  • Pizza peel or cutting board
  • Knife and cutting board

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Set to 475°F (245°C) and place your pizza stone or baking sheet inside to preheat.
  2. Prepare Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and brush with olive oil.
  3. Assemble Pizza:
    • Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
    • Top with mozzarella, feta, and crumbled sausage.
    • Add roasted red peppers, olives, red pepper flakes, and oregano.
  4. Bake: Transfer the pizza to the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  5. Finish: Drizzle with a bit of olive oil, slice, and serve hot!
